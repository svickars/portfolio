Hi, I’m Sam. I like to tell visual stories using data, graphics, and interactivity. I would love to work with you.

You can see some of my work below and if you want to get in touch, you can email me at sam.vickars@gmail.com or call me at 604-379-4890 — I’m almost always down for a chat. If you add me on Twitter, @samvickars, I'll probably follow you back.

I have a bachelor’s degree from the University of Waterloo School of Architecture and a Master’s of Science in Data Visualization from Parsons in NYC, which is pretty cool I think. While I was working on those, I lived, worked, and studied in Toronto, New York, London, and Rome. At the moment, I’m working on freelance projects and some personal stuff, but I’m always up for new work and opportunities.

I’m also an eight-time local pub trivia champion, along with my twin brother; the scoring champion in one of the lowest- division adult hockey leagues in the city; a relatively accomplished wassailer; and an early pioneer of the Circle Line Pub Crawl.

You could say that I’m single and ready to mingle.

It’s so nice to meet you.
