On Drinking
April 2017
ondrinking.com.
Adobe Awards 2017, Semifinalist

Done for William Bevington’s Typography and Visual Design, this project is made up of 196 assets, each including a glass icon, a drink name, and various other data. The assets are combined to make three separate posters showing beers, wines, and cocktails, a 24-page monograph making use of Pete Brown’s Man Walks Into A Pub, and a fully interactive mobile site, ondrinking.com.
