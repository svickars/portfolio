Hi, I’m Sam.

I tell stories through visualizing data — making art out of information. This involves creating and designing maps, building interactive tools and experiences, and collecting and analyzing raw data and information.

I finished architecture school in the summer of 2015, living, working, and studying in Toronto, Rome, and London along the way, and promptly realized that I was no architect. So I took what I learned and moved on to bigger and (some would say) better things, graduating from Parsons School of Design in NYC just this past May with a Master’s of Science in Data Visualization.

At the moment, I’m working on freelance projects and some personal projects, involving sports data, gender stereotypes, geographical connections, and really anything you can throw at me. You could say that I’m single and ready to mingle.

I’d love to work with you. Get in touch here.
